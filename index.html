About

I am a Ph.D. student for computer science at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Mads Dam and Assoc Prof. Roberto Guanciale.

My research work focuses on formal modelling and verification of low-level hardware and software such as pipelined processors and I/O devices, information flow analysis, and microarchitecture security.

Publication

“Formal Verification of Correctness and Information Flow Security for an In-Order Pipelined Processor”, FMCAD'23, Ning Dong, Roberto Guanciale, Mads Dam, Andreas Lööw.

“Foundations and Tools in HOL4 for Analysis of Microarchitectural Out-of-Order Execution”, FMCAD'22, Karl Palmskog, Xiaomo Yao, Ning Dong, Roberto Guanciale, Mads Dam.

“Refinement-Based Verification of Device-to-Device Information Flow”, FMCAD'21, Ning Dong, Roberto Guanciale, Mads Dam.
