About

I am a third-year Ph.D. student for computer security at KTH Royal Institute of Technology, supervised by Prof. Mads Dam and Assoc Prof. Roberto Guanciale.

My research work focuses on formal modeling and verification of low-level hardware and software such as processors and I/O devices, information flow analysis, and microarchitecture security.

“Refinement-Based Verification of Device-to-Device Information Flow”, FMCAD'21, Ning Dong, Roberto Guanciale, Mads Dam.
